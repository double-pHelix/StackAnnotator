Page Help

How to Ask for a Youtube Video

Simply highlight a word with your mouse to activate our annotation menu. In the menu you can select from a few video types; details (ie. explanations, descriptions), tutorials (ie. "how to videos", walkthroughs) and usages (ie. demonstrations, examples).

How to Annotate with a Youtube Video

At the bottom of the annotation menu is a button that will direct you to the annotation form. Enter in a valid youtube video (by copy/pasting) into the form and submit.
